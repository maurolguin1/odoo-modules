Sale Order Line Discount Compute

Compute the discount according to sale price change

Odoo let the seller update the sale price of directly on every sales order lines. It is also possible to allow discounts on sales order lines. In some business case, when the seller make a discount he just want to setup a new sale price without the need of calculate discount but still want to valuate his discount.

This module try solve this business case by replacing the price_unit field by a new x_price_unit field and compute automatically the discount according to the price change.

You must activate Allow discounts on sales order lines in Sales > Settings
